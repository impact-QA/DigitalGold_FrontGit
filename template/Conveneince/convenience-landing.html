Convenience
Being an investor shouldn't take up all of your time. OneGold strives to make the entire process as we can. We accept several payment options, provide a quick buying and selling process, offer true redemption for physical products, allow you to set up recurring buys and provide you with historical and real-time charting information.
Range of Payments
Once you have created your free account, you are ready to start trading immediately, We accept range of payment options let you choose how to invest. You can purchase products using a credit card, Bitcoin, wire transfer, your pre-funded OneGold Account or you can link your bank account.

With our flexible funding options,you can buy at any time when the price is right for your investing needs. You can either check out as you go, similar to any online retail site, or pre-fund your account so the funds are ready whenever you are.
Buying & Selling
Not only do we want you to be able to buy and sell at your convenience but we understand the desire to settle quickly as well. Your OneGold account represents your actual holdings and we make it easy to cash out at any time. When you are ready to withdraw funds, you can choose to recieve a physical check or a deposit to your linked bank account.

The OneGold platform can be used on any device, from your desktop to your mobile phone, allowing you to invest where and when you want. All prices are updated in real time so you can compare and shop on a go.
Online Redemption
One of the most unique aspect of OneGold is our physical redemption program. Since all digital OneGold products are backed by real assets, you are able to redeem your funds for coins, bars or rounds whenever you choose. Simply select from a variety of Gold and Silver products, confirm your shipping address and that's it. All physical OneGold orders are fulfilled by APMEX via white label dropshipping. Orders are shipped in discreet packaging, directly to you from one of the most trusted name in the precious metal industry.
Continuity Program
We want to help make investing work for you so you don't have to worry for your investments. The OneGold platform makes it easy to set up recurring buys that will automatically repeat at your chosen frequency. This allows you to build your investment, at your pace, without having to remind yourself to make a transaction.

Recurring buys can be set up for a certain dollar value or product amount. No matter how the gold or silver market moves, our system will autmatically credit your account or other selected payment method. Make investing easy with OneGold.
